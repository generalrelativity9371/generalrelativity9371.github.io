Initiative

Mount Sinai

As an FTC robotics team, we recently had the opportunity to make a difference in our community by donating toys to a local children's hospital. So, we started a project to 3D print toys for kids in the hospital. The project aims to bring joy and comfort to pediatric patients at Mount Sinai Hospital through the creation of customized and personalized 3D printed toys. With the use of 3D printing technology, the project seeks to improve the hospital experience for children and provide them with a sense of normalcy during their hospital stay.
